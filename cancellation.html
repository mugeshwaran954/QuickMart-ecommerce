Cancellation Policy

At QickMart, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction. If you need to cancel an order, please review our policy below.

Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be canceled within 30 minutes of placing the order.
  • Cancellation is not allowed once the order is shipped.
  • If an order is still in the processing stage, you can cancel it from your account.
  • Refunds (if applicable) will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Refund Policy

  • If a canceled order is eligible for a refund, the amount will be credited to your original payment method.
  • Refund processing times may vary based on your payment provider.

Exceptions

  • Perishable items such as fruits and vegetables are non-cancellable once packed.
  • Special discounts or promotional orders may have separate cancellation terms.
